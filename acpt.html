動物持續注意力測驗 (ACPT) Animal Continuous Performance Test (ACPT)

ACPT 是一套以視覺刺激為基礎的電腦化注意力評估工具，適用於小動物（大鼠、小鼠）的認知行為研究與藥效評估。整合 StEEG 腦電系統可同步記錄行為與腦電數據。

ACPT is a computerized visual attention assessment tool for small animal cognitive behavior research and pharmacological efficacy evaluation. Integration with StEEG EEG systems enables synchronized behavioral and EEG recording.
